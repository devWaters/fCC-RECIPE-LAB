Firecracker Beef and Brussels

This is my goto meal prep recipe. It's easy to make, and have a ton of protein. Quick, simple, delish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 2-3 pablano peppers
  • 2 pounds brussel sprouts
  • 3 cups brown rice
  • 1 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup corn starch slurry

Instructions

  1. Prepare your peppers and brussels
    2. In a oiled baking sheet, place halved brussel sprouts evenly across the sheet, season to taste, cook for 30-40 minutes at high temp.
  2. Cook rice in rice cooker (or pot)
  3. Cook ground beef in pan, dont stir too much as to get good browning
  4. Take brussels out the oven and toss them into the pan with the beef
  5. Pour in your hot sauce, then the honey, and drizzle in the slurry slowly to preferred consistency
  6. Make sure everything is coated evenly, toss in red pepper flakes
  7. Serve with a 2/1 ration of Beef and Brussels/Rice

Really, you could cook it however you want, aslong as the beef has good browning, and the sauce coats everything evenly.
